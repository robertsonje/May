Information taken from the Weather Atlas page on Milwaukee.

How to read: Record high/low is the hottest and coldest temperature that has ever been recorded within that time frame.

Average high/low is the average of the hottest and coldest temperatures recorded every day within that time frame. Mean maximum/minimum is the average of the single hottest and coldest day recorded in the same time frame.

Daily mean is the average temperature recorded within that time frame.

Average dew point is the average temperature of which air must be cooled to become dew.

Average ultraviolet index is the expected likelihood that the sun rays could potentially cause sunburn, from 0 to 11+. More information here.

The rest should be straightforward to understand.
